Lasting Impact

What was the lasting impact of US imposing sanctions on iran?

The U.S. naming of Iran as a “state sponsor of terrorism” triggered several sanctions with lasting impact on the economy of Iran, including the follows: 1)Restrictions on sales of U.S. dual use items; 2) Ban on direct U.S. financial assistance and arms sales to Iran; 3)Requirement to oppose multilateral lending; 4) Withholding of U.S. foreign assistance to countries that assist or sell arms to terrorism list countries; 5) Withholding of U.S. Aid to Organizations That Assist Iran.
