Interesting Facts about Ceres

1 .Closest dwarf planet to the Sun and is located in the asteroid belt, between Mars and Jupiter.
2. First asteroid to be discovered
3. Only dwarf planet in the inner solar system
4. Very thin atmosphere that contains water vapor
5. It has mysterious bright spots

Technical Information

Diameter: 946 KM
Distance from the Sun: 413 KM or 2.8 AU
