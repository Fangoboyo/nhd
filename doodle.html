CaRoT-7 b (Exoplanet)


This is CaRoT-7 b, it is a rocky red planet, about the size of earth. CaRoT-7 revolves around a K-Type star and takes 0.9 days to rotate it.
Facts:
1. It was discovered in 2009
2. Planet Type: Super Earth
3. Radius: 6654.742 mi
4. Time to rotate its star: 0.9 days
5. Distance from Earth: 522 light years
