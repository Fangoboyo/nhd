Light Transmission

What is Light Transmission?

Matirials Used in Light Transmission

  • Light Bulb
  • Colored clear paper

What is the purpose of this experiment?

To see how certain colors of light are transmitted and other colors are absorbed
as they pass through objects that are translucent!


Background Information

Any object will either absorb, reflect or transmit
light as it hits or interacts with the surface of the object.
